name: No camera sway or shake effects
description: User does not want camera sway, jolts, or shake — it's unnatural and causes motion sickness
type: feedback
---

Do not add camera sway, camera shake, camera jolts, or any forced camera movement effects.

**Why:** The user finds it unnatural and it can make people sick (motion sickness concern).

**How to apply:** Never implement camera bob, sway, shake, or lurch effects. Keep the camera stable — rely on audio and visual effects (lighting, screen overlays) for feedback instead of camera movement.
